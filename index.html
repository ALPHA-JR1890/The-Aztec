religion

Aztec religion was a polytheistic (belief in multiple gods) belief system that required ritual human sacrifices to keep the world in existence. One of the gods that the Aztecs believed in was the 2 faced snake god of knowledge and wind Quetzalcoatl.

🌟

Geography

The Aztec Empire was located in the valley of Mexico, surrounded by towering volcanic mountains and centered around Lake Texaco.

🎭

Entertainment

The Aztec civilization had structured ritual dances and theatrical dramas of myths, songs, and processions. These were intertwined with their religion.
